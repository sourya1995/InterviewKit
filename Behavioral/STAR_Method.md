# ? The STAR Method Template

Use this template to prepare your stories for behavioral interviews. The STAR method helps you provide a structured and impactful answer.

## ?? Situation
*Set the scene and give the necessary details of your example.*
- What was the context?
- When did this happen?
- Who was involved?

## ?? Task
*Describe what your responsibility was in ? situation.*
- What was the goal?
- What problem were you trying to solve?
- What was the expected outcome?

## ?? Action
*Explain exactly what steps you took to address the situation.*
- What did **you** do? (Focus on your specific contributions, not just the team's).
- How did you do it?
- What tools or skills did you use?

## ?? Result
*Share what the outcome of your actions was.*
- What was the final result?
- What did you accomplish?
- What did you learn?
- **Quantify if possible** (e.g., "Reduced latency by 15%", "Saved 10 hours of manual work per week").

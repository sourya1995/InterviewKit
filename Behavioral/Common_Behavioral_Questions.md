# ? Common Behavioral Questions

Prepare 3-5 versatile stories using the STAR method that can answer these common questions:

### ?? Conflict & Teamwork
1. Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a teammate or manager. How did you resolve it?
2. Describe a time you had to work with someone who had a very different working style than yours.
3. Tell me about a time you helped a teammate who was struggling.

### ?? Failure & Growth
1. Tell me about a time you made a mistake. How did you handle it?
2. Describe a situation where you failed to meet a deadline.
3. Tell me about a time you received difficult feedback.

### ?? Leadership & Initiative
1. Tell me about a time you took the lead on a project.
2. Describe a time you went above and beyond your job description.
3. Tell me about a situation where you had to persuade others to adopt your idea.

### ?? Problem Solving
1. Describe a difficult technical challenge you faced and how you overcame it.
2. Tell me about a time you had to make a quick decision without all the information.
